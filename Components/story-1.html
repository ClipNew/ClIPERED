How We Scaled an Agency from 10 to 100+ Daily Applicants

Category: Success Story

The Challenge

Our partner agency was struggling with a low volume of inconsistent leads. Their manual process for tracking applicants was time-consuming and prone to errors, preventing them from scaling effectively.

The Solution

We implemented a three-part strategy: a high-conversion landing page, a targeted Meta Ads campaign, and a chatbot to automate initial screening. This created a powerful, automated funnel that captured and qualified leads around the clock.
