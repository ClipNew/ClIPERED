Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions, answered. Here's what you need to know before we partner up.

What's the difference between your one-time fee and the monthly costs?

My pricing plans cover a one-time setup fee for architecting and building your custom digital infrastructure. This includes the landing page, automations, and dashboards. The ongoing monthly costs (like your Facebook Ads budget or Chatmaxima subscription) are paid directly to those third-party services and are completely under your control.

How long does the setup and implementation process take?

The timeline depends on the complexity of the plan you choose. A 'Startup Architect' plan can be deployed in as little as 1-2 weeks, while more comprehensive 'Enterprise' solutions may take 3-4 weeks to fully implement, test, and optimize.

Do I need to be technical to use the systems you build?

Not at all. My primary goal is to build intuitive, user-friendly systems. I design dashboards and processes that are easy to manage, and for 'Premium' and 'Enterprise' plans, I provide dedicated training to ensure your team is confident and proficient from day one.

What kind of results can I realistically expect?

Clients typically see a significant reduction in manual administrative work, a higher volume of qualified leads, and a more streamlined applicant tracking process. The goal is to free up your team's time to focus on high-value tasks like interviewing and client relations, which directly translates to business growth.
