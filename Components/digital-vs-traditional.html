Old Agency vs. Digital Campaign

A DMW-Compliant Comparison

The recruitment landscape has evolved. While the goal remains the same—connecting qualified talent with opportunities—the methods for achieving it have been revolutionized. Both traditional and digital processes can be compliant with DMW rules, but their efficiency and scalability are worlds apart.

The Old Way: Traditional Agency Process

  • Relies on walk-in applicants and paper resumes.
  • Manual screening leads to long queues and wasted time.
  • Data is scattered, making applicant tracking difficult and prone to errors.
  • Limited reach, dependent on foot traffic and basic job postings.

The New Way: Digital Process Campaign

  • Attracts candidates 24/7 with targeted online ad campaigns.
  • Automates pre-screening with intelligent chatbots.
  • Centralizes all applicant data in a secure, searchable digital dashboard.
  • Reaches a wider, more qualified pool of candidates across the country.

Enhancing DMW Compliance Through Digitalization

A digital process doesn't just improve efficiency; it strengthens compliance. By creating a clear, digital paper trail for every applicant interaction—from the initial ad click to the final interview schedule—you build a transparent and auditable system. This ensures that every step is documented and aligned with DMW regulations, protecting both your agency and your applicants.
